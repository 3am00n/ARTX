proposal

proposal got stuck in my throat. immediately, mental threads were tangled around business, profit, purchase, property. i can't propose this. i can warn of a bloody, wet birth that soaks the floors and walls, no matter the deceptive sterility, ephemerality, or digital nature of the tools used in its delivery. it puddles in youreyes until you're half-blind and stumbling.

i suggest a set of portals and stylistic protocols through which to enter a space of questioning.

1

can we use technologies of subjugation to subjugate technologies of subjugation?

2

who is recognized by machine eyes?

3

who does not[is not allowed to] exist?

4

how do these technologies pervade out spirits, bodies, hearts, dreams, and perceptions of self/other?

5

can a creative play of identity-making, identity-recognition, and identity-dissolution/declassification be liberatory?

6

if=true, why?

7

if=false, why?

8

who do we trust to know us?

I KNOW I'M PLAYING WITH FIRE. IT EXCITES ME.
